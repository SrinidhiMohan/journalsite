IJCSWS - Contact

You can join this growing group of scientific and social publications as any of the following ways:

  1. Editor
  2. Reviewer
  3. Reader


International Journal of Computer Science and Wireless Security(IJCSWS) welcome competent academicians to join us as Reviewers. Being a reviewer is a matter of prestige and personnel achievement. IJCSWS follows a strict set of guidelines while accepting the applicant as a reviewer of IJCSWS. The minimum sets of qualifications for being a reviewer of IJCSWS are listed below:



  • Should be a holder of PhD degree from a recognized university.
  • Should be an IJCSWS research author with at least a single IJCSWS publication.
  • Should be an author with a minimum of a 5 publications in reputed journals.

As we are in the way to start so many journal belongs to different disciplines and thus we are looking for expert and dedicated personals for this position.


IJCSWS Board Member benefits reduced publication charge. If you would like to become an IJCSWS reviewer, you should send the resume (CV) to the IJCSWS editor (ijcswseditor@gmail.com). You will receive an email shortly which lets you know whether you have been selected as IJCSWS reviewer or not.


If you want to get in touch with us through email. Please send your message to :ijcswseditor@gmail.com
